About

CBITMUN club was started back in 2011 by a group of debate enthusiasts with the motto to set up, encourage and endorse Model United Nations conferences. It was Model United Nations Initiative for the first time of its level in the city of Hyderabad. As years passed, it has not only succeeded in conducting a world class conference but has been witnessing a sharp increase in the number of participants with every edition. The club always stood for the quality of debate and hospitality it delivers during the conference. CBIT MUN is one of the few Model United Nations conference in the country that holds a legacy of Twelve successful editions.
