Inter Collegiate Conference

ICC 2023

CBITMUN organized the CBIT Inter-Collegiate Conference 2023 on the 14th and 15th of October in Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology. It aimed at instilling intellect among students and consisted of intense debate and dialogue. Students from all over the city took part in two international committees simulated.

ICC 2022

After redefining the legacy and organizing one of the biggest offline conferences post-pandemic in May 2022, CBITMUN organized the CBIT Inter-Collegiate Conference 2022 on the 27th and 28th of August in Chaitanya Bharati Institute of Technology. The event was approved by the institute under SPICES mandated by AICTE. Two high-octane international committees operated on high debating spirits and enthusiasm.

ICC 2021

CBIT Model United Nations conducted its Inter Collegiate Conference on 30th - 31st October 2021. Owing to the health and safety of the participants, and everyone involved, the conference was held in an online capacity. Mr. Mohan V Katarki, senior advocate at the Supreme Court of India graced the inaugural session as the Chief Guest.

ICC 2019

The Inter Collegiate Conference is known for the compelling and zealous debate. Upholding the same tradition, the Inter Collegiate Conference 2019 was successfully conducted on the 6th and 7th of April in CBIT. Delegates showcased some exquisite diplomacy and public policy intellect and the chairpersons pivoted the committees at a moderated pace.
